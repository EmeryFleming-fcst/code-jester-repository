- Slugline or Sceneheader: 
-- INT/EXT: Interior and Exterior
-- Setting: Where it takes place
-- Time
-- Example: EXT Beach - Towel Bar - Day
- Actions Lines
-- Capitalize character name the first time they are introduced.
-- Important props should be capitalized.
-- Sounds can be all capitalized/italicized.
- Dialogue Breaks
-- Character Name on it's own line. Dialogue lines under that. Note after in paranthesis.
-- V.O. = Voice Over
-- O.S. = Offscreen.
-- O.C. = Off Camera
- Parenthetical: Tell the actor what to do, what they're talking about, whether or not to wait a bit, etc...
-- (re: Coffee) - Explains that they are talking about coffee.
- Transitions and Cuts
-- Jump Cut, Match Cut, Jump To
